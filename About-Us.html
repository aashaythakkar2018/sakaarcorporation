About Sakaar Group

A legacy of excellence since 1963, building businesses that transform industries and enrich lives across India.

Company Overview

Founded in 1963, Swastik Oil - Sakaar Group has evolved from a small family business into one of the most respected business houses. With a diverse portfolio spanning B2B industrial solutions, distribution networks, and consumer-centric real estate developments, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses and consumers alike.

Our journey is defined by our commitment to quality, innovation, and ethical business practices. We believe in creating sustainable value for all our stakeholders while contributing to overall economic growth. Our success story is not just about business growth; it's about the lives we've touched, the communities we've uplifted and the trust we've built with our stakeholders. At Sakaar Group, we've always believed that true success lies in creating value for society while maintaining the highest standards of corporate governance and ethical business practices.

Shrivas B. Gheewala

Chairman

Vision & Mission

The guiding principles that drive our business decisions and corporate culture.

Our Vision

To be a business group that sets industry benchmarks in business excellence, innovation and sustainable practices while creating lasting value for all our stakeholders.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional value to our customers through innovative products and services that exceed their expectations.

To foster a culture of excellence, integrity, and continuous improvement across all our business verticals.

To contribute to India's economic growth while maintaining the highest standards of corporate governance and social responsibility.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We conduct our business with the highest ethical standards and transparency in all our dealings.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from products to services to customer relationships.

Innovation

We continuously seek new ways to improve and deliver better solutions to our customers.

Collaboration

We believe in building strong partnerships with our stakeholders and working together for mutual success.

Ethics

The most important pillar of our core values is ethics where customer is the core of our decisions.

Social Responsibility

We are dedicated to making a positive impact on the communities where we operate.
