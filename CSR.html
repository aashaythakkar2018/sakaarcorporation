Education Sponsorship Program

Empowering the future through education. Our CSR initiative focuses on providing financial assistance to deserving children who need support to continue their educational journey.

We believe every child deserves access to quality education, regardless of their economic background. Through our sponsorship program, we support students by covering their educational expenses based on merit and genuine need.

Contact Coordinator

Program Overview

Our education sponsorship program is designed to identify and support students who demonstrate academic potential but face financial constraints in pursuing their education.

Academic Excellence

We support students who show academic promise and dedication to their studies, helping them achieve their educational goals.

Financial Assistance

Complete or partial coverage of educational expenses including tuition fees, books, and other essential academic materials.

Community Impact

Creating a positive impact in the community by ensuring talented students don't miss educational opportunities due to financial constraints.

Eligibility Criteria

To ensure our support reaches the most deserving candidates, we have established clear eligibility criteria for our sponsorship program.

Academic Performance

Minimum 75% marks in the previous academic year with consistent academic record.

  • Strong academic track record
  • Regular attendance in school
  • Positive teacher recommendations

Financial Need

Demonstrated financial hardship that prevents continuation of education.

  • Family income below specified threshold
  • Genuine financial constraints
  • Supporting documentation required

Location Criteria

Students from Surat district and surrounding areas are given priority.

  • Surat district residents
  • Nearby districts considered
  • Valid address proof required

Age & Grade

Students from Class 1 to Class 12 and undergraduate programs.

  • Age between 7 to 21 years
  • Currently enrolled in school/college
  • Valid student ID required

Required Documents

Please ensure you have all the necessary documents ready before submitting your application for faster processing.

Academic Records

  • Latest marksheet/report card
  • Previous year's academic records
  • School/college enrollment certificate

Financial Documents

  • Family income certificate
  • Bank statements (last 6 months)
  • Employment proof of parents/guardians

Identity Proof

  • Student's Aadhaar card & passport size photograph
  • Parent/guardian ID proof
  • Address proof documents

Recommendation

  • Teacher recommendation letter
  • Principal's endorsement
  • School Admission letter with fees structure
