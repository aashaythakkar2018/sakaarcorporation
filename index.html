About Sakaar Group

Our Legacy of Excellence

Established since 1963, the industrial fuels and gas distribution vertical has grown from a small distribution business into a large trading and distribution business with a wide variety of petroleum products in liquid and gaseous forms spread across multiple industries. The new generation ventured into Real estate development vertical in 2013, giving birth to Sakaar Corporation. Since then, Sakaar Group has become a unique organisation which caters to the B2B segment in liquid fuels, B2C segment in gaseous fuels and makes thoughtfully crafted real estate developments fluffing the needs of diversified customer base. With operations spanning across multiple industries, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses and consumers alike.

Our journey is defined by our commitment to quality, innovation, and ethical business practices. We believe in creating sustainable value for all our stakeholders while contributing to the overall growth of the society.
