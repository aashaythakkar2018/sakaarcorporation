Our Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1965, Swastik Oil Company has grown to become one of the most trusted distributors and traders of premium industrial fuels. We have been distributors of GS Caltex, Indian Oil Corporation and Reliance in the past and are currently Industrial Lube Distributors of Hindustan Petroleum Corporation since 2004 and have been smoothly functioning with the company since then.

Core Competencies:

  • Vast experience has led to technical seniority.

  • Technical support of sample testing to each and every client.

  • Strong distribution fleet, leading to reduced lead time for supplies.

  • Try to solve the pain point of the customers hence building long term relationship with the clients.
